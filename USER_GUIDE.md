# OralLex User Guide

Welcome to OralLex. This tool is designed to make documenting your expertise as easy as having a conversation. 

## The Recording Session

When you are ready to document a process (like "How to deploy our microservices" or "How to handle a refund request"), open the OralLex session link.

1. **Start Speaking**: Simply hit record and start explaining the process out loud. Imagine you are teaching a new hire.
2. **The Transcript**: On the left side of your screen, you'll see your words transcribed in real-time. 
3. **The Question Queue**: On the right side of the screen, OralLex will occasionally pop up a question.
   - For example, if you say *"Normally, we deploy on Fridays"*, OralLex might ask: *"Are there any edge cases where you would NOT deploy on a Friday?"*
   - You don't need to click anything to answer. Just look at the question, say *"Oh, right, to answer that... if there is a major holiday, we hold off,"* and keep going. OralLex will understand that you've answered the prompt.

## Post-Session Review

Once you click "End Session":
- OralLex will take a few moments to compile your transcript and your answers to the Socratic questions.
- It will automatically generate beautifully formatted SOPs, FAQs, and Decision Trees.
- You will be presented with a **Session Replay** screen. Here, you can quickly scan the generated documents. If you notice something is wrong, you can highlight the transcript text, add an annotation (like *"Make this step a warning"*), and the documents will regenerate instantly.

## Accessing Your Knowledge

All generated artifacts are automatically published to your connected Notion database. Additionally, because OralLex is integrated into AgentOS, any other AI agents operating in your workspace will immediately have access to the rules and procedures you just explained.
